Compatibility Notes

Full animation support: Gmail (web & mobile), Apple Mail, Yahoo Mail, Samsung Mail, most webmail clients.
Shows first frame only (still looks good): Outlook desktop (Windows). The logo displays as a clean static image — no broken layout.
Tip: For production use, host the animated GIF on your website and reference it by URL. Base64-embedded GIFs work in previews but may be stripped by some email clients due to file size.
